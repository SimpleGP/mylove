Hello my love, I know I'm not close enough to give you a letter in person,
but at least I can write to you like this. You know I'm not always good at explaining myself,
but believe me, if you were in my head for a moment, you'd see
that you're always there, I'm always thinking of you.

I can't stop imagining a future by your side. Finish college, get a job,
and when I have enough money, go where you are to spend time with you:
watching movies, playing games, hugging endlessly.

Every time you say you love me or send a text message, my heart races.
You make me feel so loved, and I want to do everything I can to return that happiness.

I don't know if it sounds too cheesy, hahaha, but I love you with every part of my being.
I want to be with you forever, my love.
